A Message From Kris...

Thank you so much for visiting my website. Whilst having a huge love for Star Wars, after my journey around the world I found my love for perfectly made, fresh coffee. Echo 3 is my way of bringing freshly ground coffee, roasted in store to our wonderful customers along with home-baked snacks!

Here at Echo 3, I wanted to give my customers a taste of real coffee, coffee made with a lot of passion and care without paying huge prices. We also try to cater to everyone we possibly can by offering Vegan-friendly snacks, different kinds of milk and many more.

So far, we have had a fantastic response from the local community and we plan to be here for many years to come!
